I am a designer and Postdoctoral researcher at AiTech, TU Delft (NL). My current research, at the intersection of design studies, AI and robotics, is focused on understanding and designing responsible human-technology interactions. I hold a PhD in "Production, Managment and Design" from Politecnico di Torino (IT, 2018). Prior to this position, I was research fellow at AMS institute (NL, 2018-2019) and visiting research fellow at X-Studio, Academy of Art and Design, Tsinghua University (CN, 2016-2017).
